### About

**Creating** has always been one of the things I enjoy the most. I also enjoy ordering, cleaning, structuring and improving things. On the other side, I hate doing this on things that have no impact or are meaningless, though I might do it from time to time if is needed to.

The art of creating leads me to like engineering and, by consequence, software. Developing useful and **reliable** systems is what I enjoy the most regarding this subject. I usually don't like to work on software that no one will use since I think software and all engineering products should **bring value to people** at the end of the day.
